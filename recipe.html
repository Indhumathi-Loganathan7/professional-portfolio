Classic Creamy Pasta

Simple, quick, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Pour in cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese and stir until melted.
  5. Drain pasta and toss it in the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with fresh basil on top.
